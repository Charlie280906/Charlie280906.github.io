On Friday 19th December 2020, Supernatural broadcast it's final 3 episodes ever and the final thing said to all the fans out there was:
Thank you for all your support
Those were the final words heard from the Winchester brothers!
What was said by Jared and Jensen after they finished filming:

"Since we started filming the pilot, it's the longest stretch we've had without being Sam and Dean," Padalecki says of the break, which began this March when production on the CW drama paused due to COVID-19. "It's like riding a bike," Ackles says of returning to film the show's final two hours. "I feel like we've been playing these characters for so long, they're such a part of who we are, I think five years could've gone by and we would've laced up our boots and gotten in the car exactly the same as we always have."
